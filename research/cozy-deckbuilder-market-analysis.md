## Executive Summary

The **cozy deckbuilder** market represents an emerging hybrid opportunity at the intersection of two growing genres:

## 7. Key Recommendations

### Market Positioning
**"The cozy deckbuilder for players who want depth without stress"**

- Target: Slay the Spire fans wanting lighter experience
- Target: Cozy gamers wanting strategic challenge
- Differentiation: Wholesome aesthetic + mechanical depth

### Success Factors
1. **Balance depth vs. accessibility**
   - Deep enough for deckbuilder fans
   - Chill enough for cozy gamers

2. **Visual identity**
   - Soft color palette
   - Cute but not childish
   - Clear card readability

3. **Narrative integration**
   - Light story (not Griftlands-heavy)
   - Character progression between runs
   - Emotional stakes without stress

4. **Steam page optimization**
   - Target 50K+ wishlists pre-launch
   - Demo essential (cozy genre expectation)
   - Trailer shows both cozy vibe AND strategic depth

### Risk Mitigation
| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| **Too casual for deckbuilder fans** | Ensure mechanical depth |
| **Too complex for cozy gamers** | Gentle onboarding, difficulty options |
| **Genre confusion** | Clear marketing: "cozy deckbuilder" |
| **Slay the Spire comparison** | Differentiate on aesthetic + tone |
