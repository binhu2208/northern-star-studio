# Ember Character Specification

## Overview
- **Name:** Ember
- **Theme:** Anger & Redemption
- **Emotion Family:** Fire
- **Arc:** From rage to responsible use of power

---

## Character Backstory

A young adult who once burned bridges with everyone they loved. Their anger was justified — abandonment, injustice, being overlooked — but it consumed them. Now they must learn that anger can be a tool for change, not destruction.

Ember's journey is about understanding that the fire within them is neither good nor bad — it's a matter of how they choose to wield it.

## Visual Design

### Color Palette

| Role | Color | Hex |
|------|-------|-----|
| Primary (Ignition) | Bright Orange-Yellow | `#FF6414` |
| Secondary (Blaze) | Intense Red-Orange | `#FF320A` |

| Highlight | Pale Fire | `#FFFF96` |

### Visual Elements
- **Fire Aura:** Animated flame effects emanating from character
- **Hair:** Flowing, flame-like hair that shifts with emotional state
- **Eyes:** Transition from intense fiery glow to warm, controlled ember
- **Scars:** Subtle burn marks suggesting past destruction
- **Posture:** Initially hunched/guarded → upright/confident

### Expression Progression
- **Ignition:** Furrowed brow, clenched jaw, intense stare
- **Blaze:** Eyes wide, mouth open (shouting/screaming)
- **Scorched Earth:** Hollow eyes, downcast, shame
- **Smoldering:** Eyes down, flickering with guilt
- **Controlled Burn:** Steady gaze, calm expression
- **Ember:** Warm smile, eyes gentle but alive with inner fire
