# Maya Chen — Character Specification

## Emotion Cards Game Asset

---

## 1. Character Overview

**Name:** Maya Chen  
**Age:** 34  
**Profession:** Ceramicist / Pottery Studio Owner  
**Role:** Main Character — The Estranged Sibling

---

## 2. Backstory Summary

Maya Chen inherited her grandmother's pottery studio three years ago after a bitter argument with her younger brother Leo at their grandmother's funeral shattered their relationship. She hasn't spoken to Leo in five years.

She arrives at the **Garden of Echoes** — a mysterious place where emotions take physical form — carrying unresolved feelings about family, burden, and loss. Her journey involves confronting her resentment, discovering the truth about what happened, and ultimately choosing her path: reconciliation, acceptance, or righteous anger.

---

## 3. Visual Design Notes

### Appearance

- **Build:** Slender, medium height — someone who spends hours at the pottery wheel
- **Age appearance:** Early-mid 30s, soft features carrying recent stress
- **Hair:** Dark brown/black, shoulder-length, often tied back in a practical bun or loose ponytail (works with clay)
- **Skin tone:** Warm medium — reflects time in a sunlit studio
- **Eyes:** Expressive, slightly tired — carries sleepless nights
- **Notable features:** 
  - Small clay stains on hands (permanent habit)
  - Simple stud earrings — grandmother's old jewelry
  - Wears comfortable, earth-toned clothing suitable for studio work

### Color Palette

| Role | Color | Hex |

| Primary | Terracotta Clay | #C67B5C |
| Secondary | Warm Cream | #F5E6D3 |
| Accent | Studio Green | #7A9E7E |

| Highlight | Glaze White | #FFF8F0 |

### Personality Indicators

- **Default expression:** Guarded, thoughtful — walls up but not cold
- **Body language:** Closed posture initially, opens as emotional journey progresses
- **Hands:** Often fidgeting with something tactile (clay, beads, fabric)

### Design Vibe

- **Art style:** Warm, slightly stylized — cozy game aesthetic
- **Reference:** Studio Ghibli character simplicity + modern indie game polish
- **Mood:** Emotionally complex but approachable; not intimidating

## 4. Expression Guide

### Expression: Neutral (Default)
- **Use for:** Character selection, idle state, beginning of journey
- **Eyes:** Half-lidded, looking slightly down or away
- **Mouth:** Slight neutral line, neither smile nor frown
- **Eyebrows:** Slightly furrowed — carrying mental burden
- **Overall mood:** Wary, guarded, processing

### Expression: Happy
- **Use for:** Positive card moments, reconciliation paths, warmth breakthroughs
- **Eyes:** Slight crinkle at corners, genuine warmth
- **Mouth:** Gentle smile — one side slightly higher
- **Eyebrows:** Relaxed, slightly raised
- **Overall mood:** Hopeful, vulnerable, open
- **Note:** This expression should feel earned — not her default state

### Expression: Concerned
- **Use for:** Confrontation moments, difficult memories,Shadow-dominant scenes
- **Eyes:** Wider, focused, slight furrow above
- **Mouth:** Pressed line or slight frown
- **Eyebrows:** Knit together, worried
- **Overall mood:** Anxiety, fear, doubt — the weight of unresolved feelings

## 6. Design Notes

### Why This Look

Maya's design reflects:
1. **Her craft:** Earthy tones, practical clothing — someone who works with hands
2. **Her emotional state:** Warm colors but muted — carrying grief, not devoid of hope
3. **Her journey potential:** Can shift warmer (reconciliation) or cooler (acceptance) based on player choices

### Growth Potential

The character design should support visual evolution:
- Early game: More closed posture, cooler/muted palette
- Mid game: Slight warming, more expression variety
- Late game (reconciliation): Warmer tones, open posture
- Late game (righteous anger): Sharper edges, stronger colors
