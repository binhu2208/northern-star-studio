## Overview

Three new characters expanding the Emotion Cards universe, each exploring a distinct emotional theme that complements Maya's reconciliation arc.

---

## Character 1: Ember

**Theme:** Anger & Redemption  
**Emotion Family:** Fire  
**Arc:** From rage to responsible use of power

### Character Background
A young adult who once burned bridges with everyone they loved. Their anger was justified — abandonment, injustice, being overlooked — but it consumed them. Now they must learn that anger can be a tool for change, not destruction.

### Emotional Journey
1. **Ignition** — First spark of righteous anger
2. **Blaze** — Uncontrolled fury, burning everything
3. **Scorched Earth** — Realization of what the anger cost them
4. **Smoldering** — Guilt and shame, wanting to change but fearing they'll only hurt again
5. **Controlled Burn** — Learning to use anger strategically, with purpose
6. **Ember** — Sustained, manageable heat — anger as a tool, not a master

### Card Concepts (~20 cards)

**Ignition Phase (Cards 1-4):**
- Spark Strike — Quick damage, builds heat
- Flashpoint — First hit deals bonus damage
- Tinder — Setup card, prepares bigger combo
- Quick Fuse — Draw card, gain anger faster

**Blaze Phase (Cards 5-10):**
- Wildfire — Damage to all enemies, hard to control
- Heat Wave — Progressive damage each turn
- Backdraft — Damage returned to attacker
- Inferno — High damage, costs HP
- Smoke Screen — Evasion, but obscures vision
- Burning Bridges — Destroys resources, hurts self

**Scorched Earth Phase (Cards 11-15):**
- Ash Fall — Heavy damage, leaves lingering effects
- Scars — Defensive card representing past wounds
- Regret — Heal, but only after dealing damage
- Cooling Embers — Reduce anger, gain defense
-废墟 (Ruins) — Sacrifice for major payoff

**Controlled Burn Phase (Cards 16-20):**
- Bonfire — Community-focused, grows stronger with allies
- Forge — Transform anger into creation
- Controlled Burn — Predictable, sustainable damage
- Tempered Steel — Convert anger to protection
- Phoenix — Rise from ashes, convert destruction to rebirth

## Character 2: Wren

**Theme:** Grief & Memory  
**Emotion Family:** Shadow  
**Arc:** Learning to carry loss without being consumed by it

### Character Background
Someone who lost someone dear — not through death, but through change. The person they loved is still alive, but they're not the same anymore. Maybe they grew apart, maybe they changed, maybe the relationship simply ended. The grief is for someone who's still breathing.

### Emotional Journey
1. **Denial** — Pretending nothing changed
2. **Weight** — The heaviness of absence
3. **Haunting** — Memories that won't let go
4. **Bargaining** — "If I just tried harder..."
5. **Shadows** — Accepting the loss exists, but struggling to move
6. **Wren** — A small bird that carries songs from the past, not weighed down but carrying memory forward

### Card Concepts (~20 cards)

**Denial Phase (Cards 1-4):**
- Pretend — Ignore damage for a turn
- Everything's Fine — Heal, but delay the cost
- Photo Album — Draw cards, but gain no benefit yet
- Ghost — Summon a memory (pet token) that fades

**Weight Phase (Cards 5-10):**
- Heavy Heart — Gain defense, but lose speed
- Anchor — Prevent movement, protect what's precious
- Sinking — Take damage to remove debuffs
- Gravity — Pull everything down
- Burden — Trade HP for card advantage
- Stone — Become immovable, but slow

**Haunting Phase (Cards 11-15):**
- Memory Strike — Damage based on cards played this fight
- Echo — Repeat the previous card effect
- Phantom Pain — Delayed damage
- Reminiscence — Look at top deck, put back
- Hallucination — Random effect, potentially good or bad

**Shadows to Wren Phase (Cards 16-20):**
- Acceptance — Transform a negative into positive
- Flight — Escape harm, leave weight behind
- Songbird — Small, persistent, meaningful damage
- Legacy — Cards that gain power from "memory" tokens
- Carry Forward — Transform grief into strength, heal based on cards played

## Character 3: Tide

**Theme:** Trust & Vulnerability  
**Emotion Family:** Storm  
**Arc:** Learning to open up after being hurt, without losing self

### Character Background
Someone who built walls after betrayal. They were hurt — deeply — and decided it's safer to be alone. But isolation has its own cost. They must learn that vulnerability isn't weakness, and that letting others in doesn't mean losing yourself.

### Emotional Journey
1. **Storm Front** — Building walls, pushing away
2. **Thunder** — The anger of "how could they"
3. **Downpour** — Overwhelmed by emotions they can't contain
4. **Electric** — High tension, ready to strike at any sign of threat
5. **Aftermath** — Exhaustion, the walls starting to crack
6. **Tide** — Flowing, adaptable, knows when to push and when to receive

### Card Concepts (~20 cards)

**Storm Front Phase (Cards 1-4):**
- Shield Wall — Strong defense, blocks ally support too
- Lightning Rod — Redirect damage to attacker
- Barometer — Predict the next attack
- Isolation — Gain power when alone

**Thunder Phase (Cards 5-10):**
- Crack — Quick counterattack
- Thunderclap — Stun enemy, push them away
- Static — Disrupt enemy combos
- Voltage — Build charge for bigger hits
- Surge — All-in attack, leaves vulnerable after
- Short Circuit — Self-damage when attacked

**Downpour Phase (Cards 11-15):**
- Drowning — Take damage to deal damage
- Flood — Affects all, unpredictable
- Riptide — Pull enemies in, can't escape
- Soaked — Debuff that builds when attacking
- Rain Check — Delay, defer, survive

**Aftermath to Tide Phase (Cards 16-20):**
- First Drop — Small trust, opens bigger possibilities
- Ripple — Small actions that spread
- Dew — Gentle healing
- Monsoon — All-in, trust everything
- Ocean — Ultimate card: flow, adapt, contain multitudes

## Card Synergies

### Cross-Character Combos
- **Ember + Tide:** Fire meets Storm = explosive combos, charge up quickly
- **Wren + Ember:** Shadow cools Fire, controlled burn mechanics
- **Wren + Tide:** Memory supports vulnerability, grief becomes acceptance

### Emotion Family Synergies
- **Fire + Storm:** Aggressive, high-risk high-reward
- **Shadow + Warmth (Maya):** Defensive, sustain-focused
- **All four families:** Can build hybrid decks
