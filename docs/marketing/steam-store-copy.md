## Full Description

Welcome to a world where emotions are the ultimate power.

In **Emotion Cards**, you embark on a deeply personal roguelike journey through the landscape of human feeling. Every card in your deck represents an emotion, and how you play them determines your fate.

**Core Gameplay:**
Build your deck from a unique pool of emotion cards, each with distinct abilities and synergies. Play cards to deal damage, heal, buff, or debuff—but beware, mixing emotions incorrectly can backfire. Find powerful combos between joy-powered attacks, fear-based defenses, rage-fueled strikes, and love-imbued heals.

**Roguelike Progression:**
No two runs are alike. Each playthrough features procedurally generated encounters, random card rewards, and varying boss challenges. Unlock new cards, artifacts, and starter decks as you progress.

**Strategic Depth:**
Emotion management is key. Some cards gain bonuses when combined with specific emotions. Others trigger powerful effects based on your current emotional state. Master the delicate balance between your emotional resources to survive increasingly difficult encounters.

**Key Features:**
- Over 100 unique emotion cards to discover and master
- Multiple playable characters, each with their own emotional specialty
- Challenging roguelike runs with permanent progression
- Deep strategic gameplay with emotion synergy mechanics
- Beautiful art style that brings emotions to life

Will you harness the full spectrum of human emotion, or let your feelings consume you?
