## Key Feature Bullets

- **100+ Emotion Cards** — Discover cards representing joy, anger, sadness, fear, love, and more
- **4 Emotion Families** — Warmth, Shadow, Fire, Storm — each with unique synergies
- **Resonance System** — Chain 3+ same-family cards for powerful combo effects
- **Procedural Roguelike Runs** — No two runs are alike; new challenges every time
- **Character-Driven Narrative** — Guide Maya through grief, reconciliation, and self-discovery
- **Emotional Path Choices** — Different decks unlock different story outcomes
- **Accessible Yet Deep** — Cozy tone with genuine strategic challenge
