## Recommended Platforms

| Platform | Priority | Why |
|----------|----------|-----|
| **Discord** | 🔴 Primary | Direct community, beta testing, feedback loops |
| **Twitter/X** | 🔴 Primary | Devlogs, announcements, indie game network |
| **TikTok** | 🟠 Secondary | Short gameplay clips, viral potential |
| **Instagram** | 🟠 Secondary | Visual art, aesthetic branding |
| **YouTube** | 🟡 Tertiary | Devlogs, trailers, gameplay deep-dives |

### Platform-Specific Notes

- **Discord** — Most critical for indie. Build a community before launch. Use for beta access, playtesting, polls.
- **Twitter/X** — Best for dev-to-dev networking, #indiedev, #gamedev hashtags, connecting with streamers.
- **TikTok** — High viral potential for gameplay moments, card reveals. Younger audience, lower conversion but strong awareness.
- **Instagram** — Strong for art assets, character reveals, aesthetic cohesion. Less engagement for gameplay.
- **YouTube** — Long-form devlogs, "making of" content. Best for SEO and discoverability over time.

### Trending/Niche Combos

- #CardsAndCoffee — Cozy gaming vibe
- #Solitairpg — If emphasizing strategy
- #NarrativeDeckbuilder — Story-focused angle
- #EmotionalGaming — Unique angle on the game theme
- #CozyGames — Cozy game community

### Hashtag Strategy

1. **Mix of sizes** — 2 large (#IndieGame), 4 medium (#Deckbuilder), 4 niche (#EmotionalGaming)
2. **Relevance first** — Don't spam unrelated tags
3. **Rotate** — Don't use same combo every post
4. **Track** — Note which tags drive traffic

## Posting Frequency

### Pre-Launch (Current Phase)

| Platform | Frequency | Best Times |
|----------|-----------|------------|
| Discord | 3-5x/week | Anytime (community driven) |
| Twitter/X | 1-2x/day | 9am-12pm, 5pm-8pm local |
| TikTok | 3-5x/week | 6pm-9pm, 12pm-3pm |
| Instagram | 3-4x/week | 11am-1pm, 7pm-9pm |
| YouTube | 1-2x/month | Weekdays 3pm-6pm |

### Post-Launch

| Platform | Frequency | Notes |
|----------|-----------|-------|
| Discord | Daily | Community-first, keep active |
| Twitter/X | 1-2x/day | Maintain momentum |
| TikTok | 4-5x/week | Algorithm rewards consistency |
| Instagram | 4-5x/week | Stories + Posts |
| YouTube | 2-4x/month | Updates, guides, community content |

### Content Mix Recommendations

- **40%** — Game content (card reveals, gameplay clips, art)
- **30%** — Dev process (behind the scenes, struggles, wins)
- **20%** — Community (polls, questions, UGC reposts)
- **10%** — Engagement (reply to comments, other devs, trends)
