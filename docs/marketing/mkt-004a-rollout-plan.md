## Go / No-Go Risks

### 🚫 NO-GO: Launch Build Unstable
**Criteria:** Build crashes, breaks core loop, or has blocker bugs at T-2 weeks  
**Impact:** Cannot capture clean footage; players get broken experience  
**Mitigation:** QA smoke test at T-1 week; hotfix path ready; may need to delay launch date

### 🚫 NO-GO: Store Page Incomplete
**Criteria:** Missing trailer, wrong screenshots, incorrect pricing, no store copy  
**Impact:** Poor conversion; unprofessional first impression  
**Mitigation:** Bin owns store setup; double-check 2 weeks before launch

### 🚫 NO-GO: Core Assets Missing
**Criteria:** No trailer, no screenshots, no key art by T-4 weeks  
**Impact:** Cannot run campaign; no visual proof to share  
**Mitigation:** Yoshi owns asset timeline; backup static assets if video slips

### 🚫 NO-GO: Messaging Not Aligned
**Criteria:** Marketing copy misrepresents gameplay or tone  
**Impact:** Wrong audience attracts; confusion at purchase  
**Mitigation:** Hideo reviews all public messaging; FAQ alignment with design

### 🚫 NO-GO: No Launch-Day Coverage
**Criteria:** No one assigned to monitor/respond at launch  
**Impact:** Unaddressed bugs, unanswered questions, missed amplification  
**Mitigation:** Shig assigns launch-day owners; rotation for days 1-7

### ⚠️ WARNING: Creator/Press Pickup Light
**Criteria:** Few creators or press commit to coverage  
**Impact:** Launch lacks social proof; visibility drops  
**Mitigation:** Backup creator list; lead with strongest differentiator; "in case you missed it" beat

### ⚠️ WARNING: Messaging Unclear
**Criteria:** Players misunderstand the hook (deckbuilder vs. story game)  
**Impact:** Wrong audience; poor reviews; low conversion  
**Mitigation:** Simplify one-line pitch; lead with clearest clips; pin FAQ
