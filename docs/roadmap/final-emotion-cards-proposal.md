## Game Concept

### Core Mechanic: Emotion Cards

Cards represent **feelings and memories**:
- **Joy** — Draw cards, gain energy
- **Grief** — Powerful effect, but damages your "heart"
- **Memory of Home** — Heal, but adds nostalgia cards to deck
- **Regret** — Discard to undo last action

**Strategic Layer:** Balance emotional health (like HP) while building your deck of experiences.

### Narrative Integration

- **Each run** = a character's emotional journey
- **Card choices** = how they process trauma/joy/loss
- **Unlocks** = new characters with different emotional archetypes
- **Ending** = determined by emotional balance, not just victory

### Target Specs

| Spec | Target |
|------|--------|
| **Playtime** | 45-90 min per run (roguelike standard) |
| **Price** | $19.99 (premium indie, aligns with comps) |
| **Platforms** | Steam (PC) day-1, Switch 6 months later |
| **Review Target** | 90%+ positive |

## Team Alignment

### John (Code) — ✅ Supports
> "Deckbuilder is the smart technical choice. Contained scope, no networking, proven market. Godot 4 is perfect for this."

**Technical Stack:**
- Engine: Godot 4.2+ (GDScript or C#)
- Data: JSON/YAML for card definitions
- Save: Simple JSON + encryption
- Audio: FMOD or Godot built-in

**Risks Identified:**
- Card interaction edge cases → Unit testing
- Balance management → Admin tools early
- UI scaling → Design for 16:9 + 4:3

### Hideo (Design) — ✅ Supports
> "Emotion cards give us the narrative depth the market wants. Faster to prototype than Metroidvania — validate core loop in weeks."

**Design Pillars:**
1. **Emotional authenticity** — Real feelings, not fantasy tropes
2. **Strategic depth** — Simple to learn, hard to master
3. **Narrative payoff** — Every run tells a story

## Why Not the Other Options?

### ❌ Open World Survival Craft (20.8% success rate)
- **Data supports it** — highest success rate
- **But:** 2-3 year timeline, massive content requirements
- **Risk:** Too ambitious for first game, team burnout
- **Verdict:** Save for game #2 when we have more resources

### ❌ Metroidvania (Yoshi's pick)
- **Artistic appeal** — hand-crafted worlds
- **But:** Silksong looming, high comparison bar
- **Risk:** Competing against Hollow Knight legacy
- **Verdict:** Strong backup option, but deckbuilder validates faster

### ❌ Friendslop Co-op (R.E.P.O. model)
- **Viral potential** — $147M market
- **But:** Trend may peak before we ship, requires networking/backend
- **Risk:** Short lifecycle (6-12 months), disposable
- **Verdict:** Doesn't fit "emotional resonance" pillar
