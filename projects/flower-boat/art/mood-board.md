# Mood Board — Flower Boat

## Aesthetic Direction
Flat illustration with warmth. Not cel-shaded, not photorealistic. Think: Unpacking meets A Short Hike meets Snufin: Melody of Moominvalley. Soft brushwork, warm palette, gentle gradients. Nothing harsh or high-contrast.

## Style Characteristics

**Do:**
- Flat shapes with subtle gradient fills (not hard shading)
- Warm light direction — morning sun, golden hour glow
- Muted backgrounds — soft cream, pale sage, dusty blue-grey
- Soft drop shadows (not hard black)
- Hand-crafted texture hints — subtle paper grain, soft brush strokes
- Color temperature shifts: warmer on subjects, cooler on background atmosphere

**Don't:**
- High contrast or harsh outlines
- Bright saturated primaries
- Photorealistic rendering
- Heavy black lines or cel-shading
- Complex gradients or lens flare effects
- UI elements competing with content (flowers and customers are the color)

---

## Social Media / Thumbnail Readability

**Target:** 3-second first impression on phone screen

- Silhouette reads clearly even compressed
- Color temperature distinguishes mood at a glance (warm sunshine = golden, rain = muted blue-grey)
- Single dominant focal point per screenshot
- Avoid: busy backgrounds, competing detail, text-heavy compositions
- Warm tones travel well on TikTok/Instagram (tested in cozy game space)

---

## Motion Principles

- Gentle fades — 400-600ms ease-in-out
- Nothing snaps or bounces
- Weather transitions: slow dissolve (600-800ms)
- Card appearances: subtle scale + fade from 95% → 100%
- No parallax-heavy effects or fast motion
- Water: gentle sway only (CSS keyframes, no physics)
