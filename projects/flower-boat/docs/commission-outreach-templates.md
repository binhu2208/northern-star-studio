## How to Use These Templates

1. **Pick your platform** — Fiverr for direct outreach to known artists, Reddit for broader posting
2. **Fill in the brackets** — budget range, timeline, specific artist names
3. **Attach or link the commission spec** — `flower-boat-godot/docs/commission-spec.md`
4. **Send** — if you have the account, send the message

If Bin has a Fiverr or Reddit account, he can post these in under 15 minutes and get responses within 24-48 hours.
