# DES-001C: Enemy Types Design

**Task:** Design 2 enemy types for Emotion Cards  
**Designer:** Hideo  
**Date:** March 16, 2026  
**Status:** Ready for Review

---

## Overview

These enemies embody what Maya is avoiding — not opposing, but reflecting her internal struggles. "Defeat as integration" means engaging with them brings growth, not just victory.

---

## Enemy Type 1: The Weight

**Concept:** A slow-moving, amorphous entity that starts small but grows in size and threat the longer you ignore it.

### Behavior
- Starts at 1 HP but accumulates "pressure" each turn it isn't engaged
- At 3+ pressure, grows larger and gains new abilities
- If engaged early (attacked), it remains small and manageable
- If ignored entirely, eventually becomes a "run-ending" threat

### Arc Symbolism
Represents the things we avoid — they don't go away, they grow. Engaging them early makes them manageable. Maya learns that avoidance amplifies problems.

### Mechanics
- **Pressure System:** 0-5 scale
- Each turn without taking damage: +1 Pressure
- At Pressure 3: "Solidify" — gains +2 attack
- At Pressure 5: "Critical Mass" — boss-tier, ends run if not defeated
- **Engagement Reward:** Defeating it while Pressure is low gives bonus "integration" tokens

---

## Enemy Type 2: The Mirror

**Concept:** A shifting, ambiguous entity that takes form based on how you engage with it — reflects Maya's fear of being truly seen.

### Behavior
- Starts as a blank, neutral form
- Changes appearance/abilities based on the *type* of card Maya plays
- If she attacks: becomes aggressive, counter-attacks
- If she avoids/heals: becomes evasive, harder to hit
- Only by *combining* approaches does it reveal its true form

### Arc Symbolism
The fear of being seen authentically — the Mirror reflects back what you show it. Maya learns that partial engagement keeps the cycle going; true transformation requires showing all of yourself.

### Mechanics
- **Reflection Tokens:** Starts at 0
- Each attack card played: +1 Reflection (gains counter attack)
- Each heal/avoid card played: +1 Reflection (gains evasion)
- At 3+ Reflection: Transforms into "Shielded Form" or "Frenzied Form"
- **Transformation:** When defeated *while holding both attack AND heal cards in hand*, reveals "True Form" — a benevolent ally that grants bonus
- **Engagement Reward:** Defeating it while having both attack and heal options = "Authenticity" bonus (free card slot or reduced cost)

---

## Design Notes

Both enemies tie into Maya's arc:
- **The Weight** — Teaches that avoidance amplifies problems; engaging early is rewarded
- **The Mirror** — Teaches that partial engagement keeps cycles going; true transformation requires showing all of yourself

The "defeat as integration" theme unifies both encounters — defeating these enemies isn't just about winning, it's about growing.
