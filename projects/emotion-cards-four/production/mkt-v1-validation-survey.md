## Part 3 — Observer Readability Checklist

*Instructions to observer: Complete during or immediately after the session. Check all that apply.*

### Card Readability

- [ ] All text on every card was legible at arm's length
- [ ] Card layout made it easy to find the key information quickly
- [ ] No jargon or unfamiliar terms that caused hesitation
- [ ] Emotion words / prompts were instantly clear
- [ ] Iconography (if any) was self-explanatory

### Component Clarity

- [ ] Setup was intuitive — tester knew what to do without prompting
- [ ] The "how to play" explanation was concise and sufficient
- [ ] Turns / rounds were self-explanatory during play
- [ ] Scoring or end conditions were clear
- [ ] All components felt purposeful — nothing confusing or "why is this here?"

### Social Signals

- [ ] It was obvious this is a social / group game
- [ ] It was clear how many players it supports
- [ ] The energy the game is meant to create was legible from the materials
