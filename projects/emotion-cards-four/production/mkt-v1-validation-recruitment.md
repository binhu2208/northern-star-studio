## 2. Screening Criteria

Use these questions in reply or a short follow-up survey (Google Form works) to confirm fit:

| Question | What It Checks |
|---|---|
| Have you playtested or regularly play board/card games in the last 6 months? | Active player status |
| What's your preferred game night size (2–4, 5–8)? | Group fit |
| Do you enjoy games where reading other players is important? | Segment A match |
| Do you enjoy games that spark conversation or reflection? | Segment B match |
| Are you available for a 60–90 minute session on [dates]? | Logistics fit |
| Do you have a stable internet connection for remote play? (if remote) | Remote feasibility |
| Any accessibility needs we should know about? | Inclusivity |

**Accept if:** Player is active in target segment, available for the session, and matches at least one of the three audience profiles.

---

## 3. Logistics

### Target Numbers
- **Total participants:** 12–20 (across all segments)
- **Session size:** 3–6 players per session
- **Sessions to run:** 3–5 sessions total

### Format
- **Remote-first** — Use Zoom or Discord video. Share a screen for rules explanation, play via a shared TTRPG凰 virtual table (Tabletop Simulator, BoardGameArena, or a simple webcam setup with physical cards mailed in advance).
- **In-person fallback** — If a local group (Edmonton area or travel-capable) is available, run one in-person session as a backup. Physical prototype preferred.

### Scheduling
- Offer 2–3 time slots across evenings or weekends (MDT evenings work well for cross-timezone).
- Book confirmed participants individually; send reminder 48 hrs before and 1 hr before.

### What to Send Participants Before the Session
- [ ] Simplified rules sheet (v1 version)
- [ ] Link to session call
- [ ] Physical card proxy pack (mail 1 week prior if remote)
- [ ] One-question pre-session poll: "What made you interested in this playtest?"

---

## 4. Incentives

**Recommended:** Amazon or Steam gift card (~$15–25 CAD per participant), or studio merch if available.

- **Individuals:** $20 CAD gift card
- **Full group (3–5 players who came together):** $15 CAD per person + studio credit
- **Post-session feedback form completed:** Additional $5 CAD bonus

> Note: Keep incentives modest — enough to show appreciation, not enough to bias feedback toward positivity.
